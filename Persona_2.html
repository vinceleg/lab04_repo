Persona 2

Ms. Joan Thanos

Age: 45

Occupation: Office Manager

Characteristics:

Ms. Thanos is a busy successful office manager for Deloitte Canada. She has been married for several decades and has two children. She is an intelligent detail and family-orientated person. As an office manager, Ms. Thanos is used to dealing with a variety of personalities and is an effective efficient communicator. Ms. Thanos is able to read and comprehend complex reading material.

Goals:

Ms. Thanos's primary goal is to advise and mentor her son while he navigates a fledling career. Ms. Thanos hopes to provide her son helpful legal advise so he act in his own interests, and career. Ms. Thanos hopes to create the basis for a meaningful pattern of communication between her and her son so that any challenges that arise can be met. Ms. Thanos hopes to provide her son with a resource for any challenges that occur outside of his career.

Needs:

Ms. Thanos needs important information to be presented in a organized and concise manner. Ms. Thanos needs a resource in order to protect her son from being exploited. Ms. Thanos needs a site that is regularly updated to provide the most up to date information for a new industry. Ms. Thanos needs a resource to which to direct her son.
